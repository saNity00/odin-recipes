Pho

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese noodle soup made with noodles, savory beef or chicken broth, thinly sliced meat, fresh herbs like cilantro and basil, bean sprouts, lime, and chili peppers

However, for this particular project, it will be a vegetarian option.

Ingredients

  • rice noodles
  • vegetable broth
  • tofu
  • mushroom

Steps

  1. prepare the noodles
  2. Make the broth
  3. Cook the tofu/mushrooms
  4. assemble the bowl
