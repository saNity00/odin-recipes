KBBQ

Description

Literally order ANY different kinds of cuts of meat you want.

Put it all the grill, and cook it yourself. Usually served with numerous traditional dishes like pickled radish, fishcake, kimchi and sometimes a nutritious soup.

Ingredients

Here are a few common ingredients for a better idea

  • brisket
  • pork belly
  • boneless short rib

Steps

  1. thin the meat or get pre-sliced cuts
  2. set up the grill, and leave it on medium high heat
  3. grill the meat using tongs
